Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal in Jaipur is known for its stunning architecture and rich history.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar in Delhi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a prime example of Indo-Islamic architecture.
